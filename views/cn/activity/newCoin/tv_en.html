Basic Information

English Name: Ti-Value

Abbreviation: TV

Official Website:http://www.t.top/

Wallet:http://www.t.top/

Blockchain:http://www.t.top/index/block.htm

Github：https://github.com/tivalueteam/TiValue

Tchat is a blockchain smart contract distributed scenario social network. In the global trend of smart mobile phone, users can use Ti-Value on smart phone like Alipay and WeChat. Ti-Value is not just a wallet, it is an integration of blockchain technology with functions like Wechat and Alipay. It is more like a decentralized social network to go from point to point and then back to the point, connecting the world of different scenarios through this link.
