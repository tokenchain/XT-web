PART 1: Overview

1.1 Launch of Hyperpay

In 2016, the world economic forum published its first blockchain report “The future of financial infrastructure: An ambitious look at how blockchain can reshape financial services”. It mentioned a few potential and promising applications of blockchain beyond bitcoin, while among the many applications mentioned, the payment field is one of the most important application scenarios for blockchain technology, and is enhanced to unprecedented position. "Blockchain + pay" concept gradually be known and accepted by everyone.
There is no uniform clearing center in the world, because no organization or institution can get the endorsement of the credibility and obtain the trust of different financial institutions, but with the advantage of the blockchain distributed ledger technology, it can solve the problem of transaction authenticity and reliability.
Based on this background, the Hyperpay project came into being. The team has developed a set of super payment plans after years of efforts, to achieve the real-time transaction and payment between BTC, ETH, Hcash and other cryptocurrencies with multi-fiat currencies, and with the current mainstream third-party payment platforms like WeChat pay, Alipay as well.

1.2 Hyperpay Foundation

i. Introduction

Hyperpay Foundation (“HPF”) is set up as a non-profit organization to pursue the principal objectives of accelerating the use of blockchain technology and development of hyperpay ecosystem. It will allow to register members, accept donations and operate in public interests relating to blockchain technology and H yperpay ecosystem. Hyperpay has been registered as a company limited by guarantee in Australia.

ii. Principles

The principles of HPF is to support and coordinate the efforts of the blockchain community by helping to create greater awareness of the benefits of the blockchain technology and better development of hyperpay project through a concerted effort in educational and awareness campaigns to promote the use of blockchain technology and its related technological requirements for enthusiasts, developers, regulators, technologists, practitioners and users globally.

iii. Activities

HPF will undertake the following activities:

- Direct and supervise the development and maintenance of the Hyperpay Tokens

- Promote the safety and harmony of the HPT blockchain ecosystem through the adoption of best practice governance principles

The management team will be responsible for:

- Publish a report on the delivery of the objectives set out in the Token Sale every quarter. The audited annual financial statements will be lodged with ACRA

- Provide reports on a regular basis to the Advisory Committee

1.3 Concept of Hyperpay

The so-called HYPER-payment, is combined the blockchain technology, mobile payment wallet and API function together to solve the digital currency, debit card, credit card, WeChat, Alipay, shopping records, medical insurance, stock, living expenses budget and all of these problems related to payment in one app.
It is an encrypted digital payment solution using globalized electronic money which integrates real-time, seamless exchange of multiple mainstream currencies as well as digital currencies, including Bitcoin, Hcash and Ethereum, among others. Additionally, Hyper Pay will provide a range of payment processing services for businesses selling online, by connecting merchants to a consolidated network encompassing global, regional and local payment methods. Hyperpay aims for growth in users, specifically to be the fastest growing online payment gateway.

1.4 Hyperpay Key differentiates

a. Built on Hcash ecosystem

With constant success of Hcash, tens of thousands of businesses, third-party payments have cooperated and will work with Hcash and Hyperpay. At present, there are many third-party international payment platforms include Hcash into their recharge channels. This is the embodiment of Hcash as a currency feature and the cornerstone of the Hyperpay payment network. With Hcash's payment network, it's easier to set up a new third-party platform than ever before.

b. Cooperated with leading payment enterprises

Hyperpay is a payment system based on the HCASH public blockchain to support a variety of digital currencies, and it has access to the WeChat payment and Alipay paid overseas clearing business, can pay all the digital currencies. At the same time， it also grafted to 1,000 businesses that Hcash has accumulated, including the largest shopping center in the southern hemisphere Chadstone Shopping Center.

c. Hyper terminal

The team will commit to the payment of terminal system development, and strive for publishing hyper terminal system in time during this year, based on cloud synchronization cashier, mobile phones and other mobile devices to check the payment.

d. Solve the fragmentation of payment scene

As a connection bridge of small and medium-sized businesses and Alipay, WeChat, Baidu wallet and other payment agencies, Hyperpay provides a payment product that can unify a variety of mainstream payment channels and payment products to solve the problem of payment scene fragmentation. It can facilitate billing management and check of the small and middle sized offline businesses, and also enhance the demands of mobile payment services that the consumers would like to pay for. In the future, we can develop a series of business models: "payment + marketing", "payment + customer management", "payment + resource management", "payment + financing", etc. And through these upgrade modes, Hyperpay can solve the problem of payment scene fragmentation.

e. Hyperpay: in clearing and settlement

• Accelerate the entire financial system. The fundamental technology structure changes, from telecommunications and ICT to ICT, to the Internet, then forward to cloud computing. Imagine that if the technical structure of the entire financial system can establish in a distributed network, all nodes share all the information, all the data, what will be the scene?

• Bring the disruptive impact of the account management system. There is no account system in the blockchain, only two password keys, a public key and a private key. More importantly, the public key and private key generated in the blockchain are not managed by the bank. The private key represents the identity, represents the ownership of the assets, through a set of encryption algorithm to replace the central institutions, to achieve privacy protection, security protection. These will bring a huge impact of the account management system.

• The digital wallet can make our payment and cross-border remittance costs close to zero.

Interface design and user experience done by world-class team; Mobile phone or email can be used on the site to receive fund; Payment by scanning barcode.

m. Security Policy

Visit to server under 24-hour monitoring; Establishment of strict administrator access; Installation of firewall; All customer must have at least a second verification method and set trading password; Suspicious log-in warning, including suspicious IP log-in; Email warning on suspicious login and 24-hour account locking function.

n. Hyperpay Bill Payment

Support household bill payment, parking bill payment, phone bill payment and credit card bill payment.
