CDN

Different applications such as Games, videos, Real time Broadcast, downloads, digital hardwares etc. have been consistently boosting up the demands for the bandwidth on internet connection. It makes the cost for bandwidth keep surging up. And yet,having a cost effective bandwidth has become a pressing need for content providers. CDN can effectively solve the problem. CDN provides services like internet speed up, data storage, cloud management etc. Today, the market size of CDN will soon reach an amazing scale of USD10 Billions.
