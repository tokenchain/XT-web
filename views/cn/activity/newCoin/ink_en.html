What is INK?

The vision of Ink is to provide Sovereign Consortium Blockchains to different use cases, to build a blockchain based infrastructure for the Creative Industry where various applications were possible and correlate to each other in one system.

Based on public blockchain (Qtum), an Intellectual Property Assets Exchange is built as a trusted corridor for cash generation and token issuance, making it an integrated ecosystem. At the same time, the cross-chain protocol enables value and information to flow freely between public blockchain and consortium blockchains.

Official website：https://ink.plus/

INK Solutions

INK SOVEREIGN CONSORTIUM BLOCKCHAIN SOLUTION FOR REGIONAL USE

Ink Sovereign Consortium Blockchain is a set of consortium blockchain developed for regional use cases with different legal frameworks and cultural backgrounds which follows the standards of Fabric. The Infrastructure for Creative Industry based on it enables developers to easily build powerful decentralised applications.

Infrastructure for Decentralised Content Production

Ink is building an Intellectual Property Assets Exchange on Qtum. This platform enables securitisation and tokenisation of Intellectual property, as a trusted corridor for cash generation and industry reshuffle in favour of artists and creators.

INK Cross-chain Protocol

For different sovereign consortium blockchains, the capability of cross-chain interaction is key to realise the network of Values. The Qtum public blockchain sitting at the very heart of Ink ecosystem, which becomes a natural bridge for transfer of value and information. It enables value and information to flow freely between Qtum chain and Ink sovereign consortium blockchains.
