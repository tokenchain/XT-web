Lever Rules

Outline

In order to increase your available funds for trading and provide a support for short selling, EXX enables a new leverage rule with the core content of "only accounting the current margin account and risks of the limited liability that the account cannot be liquidated". In order to guarantee the security of the transaction, EXX will be cautious to open the margin markets with depth and set a limit of maximum leverage times. EXX also will do some price protection measures, however, this cannot 100% protect your positions, please carefully choose the margin times.

Margin

When you trade in a specific market, you can enable Margin Trading by adding a sub-account and choose the leverage time. The system will display the available funds of the leverage times you selected ( if your borrow amount is larger than available amount of the system, you are able to borrow not larger than the system borrowing amount, which will be changed if other users use the amount ). If you enable the Margin trading, the system will borrow the funds according to the leverage times you set. When you place orders, it will priority use your funds and thereafter use the borrowing funds to place orders.

Restrictions of Enable Margin Trading

Due to the multi-currency, many currencies lack of depth or do not run stably and other reasons, they cannot participate in leverage. EXX adopts the independent sub-account model to support margin trading, which means to start leverage you need to have at least one sub-account that one account only allows to trade one market. You can go to the main account and other sub-accounts to trade other markets. In the margin account, the related currencies are not allowed to transfer-out, only to transfer-in. The other currencies do not participate accounting of liquidation risk and liquidation even if the currencies are transferred in. If the system supports the stable currency such as USDT, it will notify and possibly modify the liquidation calculation.

Fees and Repayment

1. Leverage is essentially a kind of borrowing. The system serves as a middleman to control risks and profits. Therefore, when the leverage is used and a real loan is generated, a daily borrowing interest will result in a certain borrowing expense, which will be included in the risk assessment of liquidation, But only in the repayment or closing, will be a one-time charge, the principal of interest generated, only after the use of their own funds, and the use of leveraged funds part of the funds will arise, open if you do not use Leverage part of the funds, then does not generate any interest. Without real lending, even if the lever is opened, there will be no risk of liquidation.

2. All loans that have been generated, unless you manually repay, or when the position is closed, will really repay and stop producing profits.

Leverage Times

For protection purposes, the market in principle will not provide more than 5 leverage times, the multiple refers to the actual can borrow multiple, the specific open user can freely choose the magnification, the choice of magnification will be used to limit the user's actual use loan funds will not exceed the rate, does not mean that you must, or have used the rate of funds.

Sources of Funds

The lending funds are both from merchants, in the early stage, EXX will introduce more merchants for users.

a. Closing Price

The calculation of zero personal assets when one has incurred a loan is as follows:

Amount of Sellers = Available Freezing of the Sellers Currency - Loans of the Sellers - Unsettled Interest of the Sellers.

Amount of buyers = buyer's currency available plus freezing - buyer's money borrowed - buyer's currency outstanding interest.

The price at which the asset is zero = - the amount of sellers / the amount of buyers.

Closing positions are calculated as "Floating up or Floating down" ("1-Closing ratio") "with a" zero asset value " e.g:

Risk Ratio: 81% Reminder Ratio: 80% Closing Ratio: 90% Closing Price: 100eth Risk: High

Means that this position is very dangerous, has triggered a liquidation reminder (reminded every 6 hours), up to 90% of the time to liquidate, but the liquidation price will be in the state of assets to 0 to liquidate.

If the up is the direction of liquidation, your risk factor will be the lowest if the price is 0, if the downside is the liquidation direction, when the current price is equal to or greater than twice the liquidation price, the risk coefficient is 0, and less then variety.
